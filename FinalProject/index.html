Fostering the Foster Family

Foster Indiana is a ministry in North East Indiana that seeks to foster the foster family. We realize that when a foster family gets a placement, it can really turn their house upside down. We want to help with the transition. To foster means to give practical care, nurture or promote growth/development. We would like to foster the foster family by providing free of charge...

  • Various supplies (strollers, diapers, clothing, etc.)
  • Resources to help you connect to foster care support
  • Provide a means to contact us to get help or be a part of fostering the foster families
